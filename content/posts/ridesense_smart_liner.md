## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

RideSense AI introduces the world's first **self-powered Smart Tire Liner** - a breakthrough innovation that embeds micro-sensors between bicycle tires and tubes to **predict and prevent punctures before they happen**.

Unlike reactive pressure monitors, our flexible sensor mesh detects embedded glass, nails, and thorns at 5mm depth - **before they puncture the tube**. Powered perpetually by tire compression energy, RideSense eliminates the #1 fear of India's 150M cyclists: roadside punctures.

## THE RIDESENSE SOLUTION

### Core Innovation: Smart Tire Liner with Embedded Sensor Mesh

A revolutionary **2mm flexible liner** that sits between tire and tube, containing:

#### 1. Embedded Micro-Sensor Array
- **12 pressure sensors** in circular grid pattern
- Detects uneven pressure distribution
- Maps tire surface in real-time
- Identifies embedded objects at 5mm depth (before puncture!)

#### 2. Piezoelectric Energy Harvesting
- Generates 2-5mW from tire compression
- 30 minutes cycling = 24 hours standby power
- Capacitor storage (20+ year lifespan)
- **Never needs battery replacement**

#### 3. Temperature Monitoring
- 3 strategic sensors detect overheating
- Predicts blowout risk
- Alerts to brake drag, excessive friction

#### 4. Wear Detection Layer
- Conductive traces break as liner degrades
- Shows remaining life percentage
- Predicts replacement timing

#### 5. Bluetooth Low Energy Transmission
- Sends data every 5 seconds
- 10-meter range to phone/display
- Ultra-low power consumption

### The Complete System

**Package includes:**

1. **Smart Tire Liners (2)** - Front and rear wheels
   - Universal fit (26" standard covers 80% of Indian bicycles)
   - 4 size options for complete market coverage

2. **Handlebar Display Unit**
   - E-ink screen (solar-powered, no charging needed)
   - Real-time tire pressure display

   - Doubles as bike computer (speed, distance, time)
   - GPS module for theft tracking
